Activities

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is a year-round activity in Kenya, and visibility reaches up to 30 meters; be aware that during July-August the visibility may be reduced, due to windy sea conditions. Generally, October-February is considered to be the best time to visit for diving. Water temperatures range from 23 Celsius to 28 Celsius, with the coolest times being June-September; a 3mm exposure suit is adequate protection for most. Costs Kshs 3,000/- per person/hr

Jet Skiing

This is the perfect tour for thrill seekers. You get to ride the jet-ski for 30 minutes in the open sea. You can choose to visit the Robinson island in the Indian ocean. An instructor will show you how to operate the machine and remain as your guide if you please, then it is off to the open waters. The thrill, the adrenaline jolt, is the right amount needed for you to unleash your wild side. Costs Kshs 3,000/- per person every half hour.
